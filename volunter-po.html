Volunteer Opportunities

Engage with the community by participating in various volunteer activities.

Available Volunteer Opportunities

Mentoring Current Students

Objective: Guide and support current students in their academic and career pursuits.

Requirements: Alumni with at least 5 years of experience in their field.

Time Commitment: 2-3 hours per month.

Organizing Alumni Events

Objective: Help organize and manage alumni events and reunions.

Requirements: Alumni with event planning experience.

Time Commitment: 5-10 hours per event.

Community Service Projects

Objective: Participate in community service activities organized by the alumni network.

Requirements: All alumni and students are welcome to participate.

Time Commitment: Varies by project.
